The optic nerve is the most important part of an eye which plays the role of visual information-carrying messenger. It carries information from your eyes to your brain in visual form. According to research in the USA, approximately 3 million people are suffering from this disease having ages above 40 years.

Effect of CBD oil on Macular Degeneration

Macular Degeneration is a very serious disease of the eyes particularly in the USA in which 12 million peoples are suffering from it. If it is not treated early, the patient can lose his vision permanently. Macular Degeneration is the wearing down of a small central portion of the eye's retina, called as Macula. The main function of the macula is to make our vision focus on something. We are doing all attention requiring activities with the help of macula such as reading and recognition of people faces etc. When the macula is damaged, you may experience blurry vision with a short attention span.

Different medical studies have pointed out that, CBD oil can decelerate macular degeneration by slowing down its causes. CBD oil can stop the unwanted growth of blood vessels in the eye, which is also a big reason for macular degeneration.
